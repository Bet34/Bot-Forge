## What is Junkyard Forge?

Junkyard Forge is a **no-code AI bot builder** designed for hustlers.

Instead of writing long prompts from scratch, you:
- Drag (or click) parts from the library
- Build your bot visually
- Get a ready-to-use system prompt
- Test it instantly

This version is built specifically to run inside **Replit**.

---

## How to Run

1. Open this Repl
2. Click the big green **Run** button at the top
3. The full Junkyard Forge interface will appear in the preview window on the right

That's it. No setup needed.

---

## How to Use It

### Step 1: Build Your Bot
On the left side you have 4 categories:

- **Neural Cores** — The brain (Grok, Claude, Llama, GPT, Gemini, etc.)
- **Personas** — The personality/style (Downriver Hustler, Sharp Bettor, Recovery Pro, etc.)
- **Knowledge Vaults** — What the bot knows (Repair guides, Betting data, Hustle playbooks, etc.)
- **Tools & Monetization** — Extra powers (Stripe, Replit Deploy, Affiliate, etc.)

Click any part to add it to your build.

### Step 2: Watch Your Prompt Get Forged
As you add parts, the **Forged Prompt** box on the right automatically updates with a clean, structured system prompt.

### Step 3: Test Your Bot
Click the **"TEST YOUR BOT"** button to open a chat window and talk to the bot you just built.

### Step 4: Copy the Prompt
Click **"Copy Prompt"** to copy the full system prompt.  
You can then paste it into:
- Custom GPTs
- Claude Projects
- Grok
- Any other AI tool
